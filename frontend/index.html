Fraud Detection AI MVP

Our AI-powered MVP is designed to detect fraudulent activities, fake advisors, and suspicious market manipulation in real-time. By analyzing behavioral patterns, transaction anomalies, and communication data, it ensures retail investors stay safe and informed.

AI-Powered Verify Advisor / RA / Broker

This module uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and anomaly detection models to flag fraudulent investment schemes and advisor scams before they cause damage. It is built to protect retail investors, enhance trust, and promote safe financial growth.

AI-Powered Social Media Risk Analyzer

This module scans pasted social media posts and online discussions for potential fraud signals, misleading claims, and pump-and-dump patterns. It assigns an explainable risk score with highlighted snippets so investors clearly understand the reasoning behind each flag. Designed for transparency, it helps users make safe, informed decisions online.

Corporate Announcement Credibility Checker

This module ingests corporate announcements and evaluates their trustworthiness by comparing statements with historical baselines, company records, and past performance data. It generates a credibility score and highlights potential discrepancies. A simple dashboard visualization simulates how a suspicious post could trigger a price spike, helping investors stay alert to manipulative tactics.
